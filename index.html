OPUSDEV

Browser 3D game on a tiny stylized planet — built with Three.js (WebGL) and three-mesh-bvh. Play at opusdev.org, no install.

Multiplayer

Up to 10 players per room — same Capoeira character, nicknames above heads. Connect from anywhere.

World build

Hit PLAY and the planet assembles around you — terrain, city, and foliage materialize from tumbling blocks in a shader-driven reality bubble.

Solana

OPUSDEV is a Solana-native browser game. Community token $OPUSDEV lives on pump.fun — play first, explore the planet, find us on-chain.

Works everywhere

Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge — desktop and mobile.

Controls

  • WASD — move
  • Mouse — look around (360°)
  • Space — jump
  • E — interact

Locations

  • Hills, cliffs, and open ground
  • Waterfront and wet shores
  • City streets with signs and poles
  • Trees, grass, and ring roads around the planet
